Vancouver

House of Vintage Vancouver is located in the Arts District of Downtown Vancouver, and we take pride in being a part of it. Our collection comprises multiple dealers who are always on the lookout for the best finds for you. At HOV Vancouver, we specialize in quality mid-century modern furniture and decor. In addition, we offer various vintage and antique furniture, artwork, household items, collectibles, jewelry, clothing, and accessories.
