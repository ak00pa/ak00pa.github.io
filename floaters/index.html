Floaters

What are they?

Get ready to meet the mysterious Floaters - a remarkable collection of extraterrestrial beings that defy the boundaries of imagination. These otherworldly creatures draw inspiration from Earth's diverse species while boasting unique and rare characteristics that set them apart from any lifeforms known to humanity. In this glimpse into the universe's vast tapestry of Floaters, we introduce four captivating examples of these extraordinary beings, offering a compelling taste of the infinite variety beyond our world. Step into a realm where uniqueness knows no bounds, and the Floaters await to astonish and inspire.
